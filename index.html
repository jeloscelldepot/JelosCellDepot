Frequently Asked Questions

Screen replacements and battery swaps usually take 45 minutes to 2 hours. Complex motherboard repairs or water damage diagnostics may take 1 to 3 business days.

We offer a standard 30-day warranty on all repair labor and a 90-day warranty on installed parts (excluding accidental physical damage post-repair).

We offer options for both OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts and high-quality aftermarket alternatives, allowing you to choose based on your budget.

We have two main branches: our Sto. Domingo branch located at Public Market Vacunero, and our Vigan branch at #86 Rizal Street, Pagburnayan, Brgy. 8. Check the Locations section above for exact map pins!
